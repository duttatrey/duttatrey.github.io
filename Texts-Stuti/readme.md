# स्तुति · Stuti

**Sacred texts for the wandering devotee.**

A small, clean reader for Hindu devotional texts — presented in Devanāgarī, IAST transliteration, and English translation, with no clutter and no ads. Built as a personal project to have these texts somewhere quiet and readable.

## Features

- **Three scripts** — toggle between Devanāgarī, IAST romanisation, and English at any time
- **Filter by deity** — Durgā / Devī, Hanumān, Sūrya
- **Filter by type** — Ārtī, Chalīsā, Stotra
- Readable typography (EB Garamond + Noto Sans Devanagari), designed to be used during pūjā or quiet reading
- No JavaScript frameworks, no tracking, no ads — just the texts

## This is a living site

This collection is not complete and is not meant to be — it will grow over time. The current texts centre on Durgā/Devī, Hanumān, and Sūrya, but the plan is to expand to other deities (Śiva, among others) and other forms of devotional text beyond Ārtī, Chalīsā, and Stotra.

## A note on accuracy

These texts have been sourced from various places on the internet and compiled here for personal use and ease of reading. **They may contain errors** — in the Devanāgarī, the transliteration, or the translation. I have done my best to cross-check, but I make no claim to scholarly authority.
